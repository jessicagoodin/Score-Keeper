About the Fair + Square Web App

You: 1, Them: 0

Each person has the entire world of information in their pocket, and you're still keeping score in your head?

Oh, my bad, you're using your notepad on your phone. I guess that could work...

I have a better idea. Let's open up Fair + Square and not only get to keep an accurate score, complete with team names, colors, and specific to what game you're scoring...

But let's log in with the other team and score LIVE, making sure that everyone's playing an honest game.

If you'd like, you can even keep track of your team's season. I'd say that's a win for the home team.
